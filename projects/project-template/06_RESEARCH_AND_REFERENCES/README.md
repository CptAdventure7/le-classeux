# 06 Research And References

External references and prior-art material live here.

## Folders

- `papers`: paper summaries, reading notes, and citation-backed research artifacts
- `standards`: standards notes, extracts, and compliance-relevant reference material
- `patents`: patent searches, claim summaries, and prior-art notes
- `competitor_landscape`: competitor research and market comparisons
- `cots`: component-area COTS assessments, one canonical JSON per component subfolder, and downselect material
- `web_references`: general web research that is not vendor-specific
- `presentations`: external slide decks or presentation notes used as references

## Authoring Rules

- Keep source material in the narrowest matching child folder.
- Put vendor or product assessments in `cots`, not `web_references`.
- In `cots`, use one subfolder per component area and keep original screenshots, PDFs, and similar captures in the linked SharePoint structure instead of this repository.
- Prefer updating an existing note over creating near-duplicate summaries.
